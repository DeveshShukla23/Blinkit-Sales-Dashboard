> 🚀 A professional, fully interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze
> Blinkit's grocery delivery sales performance across multiple outlet
> types, locations, and product categories.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Blinkit (formerly Grofers) is one of India's fastest-growing instant
grocery delivery platforms. This dashboard dives deep into their sales
data to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable business insights that
can drive smarter decisions for outlet managers, marketing teams, and
business strategists.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Tracked

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| 💰 Total Sales | $1.2 Million |
| 🛒 Total Items | 8,523 |
| ⭐ Average Rating | 3.9 |
| 🏪 Total Outlets | 10 |
| 📦 Avg Sales Per Outlet | $141.0 |

---

## 🔍 Dashboard Features

- **Sales by Item Type** — Which product categories generate the most revenue
- **Sales by Outlet Location** — Tier 1 vs Tier 2 vs Tier 3 city performance
- **Sales by Outlet Type** — Supermarket vs Grocery Store breakdown
- **Sales Trend (2012–2022)** — 10-year historical sales performance
- **Top Performing Items** — Fruits, Snacks, Household & more ranked by revenue
- **Interactive Filters** — Filter by Outlet Size, Location & Fat Content

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- 🏆 Tier 2 cities lead in total sales at **$0.47M**, outperforming Tier 1
- 🛍️ Supermarket Type 1 dominates with **65.54%** of total outlet sales
- 🥦 Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the **top revenue drivers**
- 📈 Sales peaked in **2018 at $205K** showing strong mid-decade growth
- 🏪 High-size outlets consistently outperform medium and small outlets
